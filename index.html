Our Story

Organic Bakery started from a humble beggining. The founder chief Chef Paris Makeda had dreams that one day the bakery would grow and become an empire. She started Organic Bakery in 2010 at her house in Nakuru. It grew so fast she opened her first shop in Nakuru town then relocated to Nairobi headquarters.

Throughout the years the bakery has grown and we are focused on delivering quality, remarkable and delicious cake to make our customers come for more. In 2015 we set our headqurters at Prestige Mall Ngong road Nairobi and we have more than 250 employees in our ten branches country wide.

Chef Makeda has a passion to mentor and equip the young generation with skills to bake that's why in line with a vision she wants to open a bakery school to help the young generation get baking skills because, Baking is therapy.
